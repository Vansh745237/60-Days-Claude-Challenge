# Day 31 – AI Supply Chain Control Tower

## 🚀 Overview

On **Day 31** of my **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I built **AI Supply Chain Control Tower**, an interactive operations management simulator that places users in the role of the **Head of Operations** for a global supply chain company.

The simulator presents real-time operational alerts and challenges players to make strategic decisions under pressure. Every action directly impacts key business KPIs such as service level, customer satisfaction, inventory health, transportation efficiency, operating cost, and revenue protection.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Simulate real-world supply chain operations.
* Practice operational decision-making under time constraints.
* Understand the impact of logistics disruptions on business performance.
* Improve strategic thinking through KPI-driven decisions.
* Learn how operational choices affect customer satisfaction, costs, and overall efficiency.

## 🚨 Operational Alerts

The simulator randomly generates scenarios such as:

* Port Congestion
* Supplier Delay
* Truck Breakdown
* Warehouse Stockout
* Customs Inspection
* Demand Spike
* Factory Machine Failure
* Weather Disruption
* Wrong Inventory Count
* Damaged Shipment

Each alert includes:

* Priority Level
* Business Impact
* Time Remaining
* Resolution Options
