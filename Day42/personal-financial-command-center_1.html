Tips, checklist & resources
The habits that matter most for mixed freelance and side income.

For freelance & side income

01Pay yourself a "salary." Move a fixed amount to a spending account monthly regardless of what came in — let the business/freelance account absorb the lumps.
02Separate tax money the day it lands. Move your estimated tax % into a locked account immediately, not at filing time.
03Size your emergency fund to your worst realistic dry spell, not a generic 3-month rule — irregular income needs more runway.
04Track reliability, not just amount. A ₹10,000 steady retainer is worth more to your stability than a ₹30,000 one-off.
05Advance tax has quarterly due dates (15 Jun, 15 Sep, 15 Dec, 15 Mar) — missing them adds interest under Sections 234B/234C.
06Keep 3 income streams alive where possible — one dry client shouldn't threaten your whole month.
