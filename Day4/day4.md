## Biggest Insight

I realized that becoming a Data Scientist is not only about learning Machine Learning algorithms. Strong foundations in Python, SQL, Statistics, Data Analysis, and real-world projects are equally important. Consistent project building and networking significantly improve career opportunities.

## Learnings

- Chain-of-Thought Prompting improves AI reasoning quality.
- Breaking a large goal into smaller milestones makes planning easier.
- Data Science requires a combination of programming, mathematics, and domain knowledge.
- Real-world projects are essential for portfolio development.
- Networking and personal branding can accelerate career growth.
