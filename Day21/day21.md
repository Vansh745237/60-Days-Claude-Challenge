# 🔍 Day 21 – Digital Privacy Dashboard with Claude

## 📌 Overview

For Day 21 of the Claude AI Challenge, I used Claude to generate a complete **Digital Privacy Dashboard** that analyzes a user's digital footprint and visualizes privacy exposure through interactive charts, heatmaps, rankings, and risk assessments.

The dashboard was built entirely using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** and provides insights into how different apps and services contribute to an individual's online presence.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project was to explore how AI can generate sophisticated analytical dashboards from a single prompt while helping users better understand their digital privacy and online exposure.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Claude AI
- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
- SVG Graphics

---

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Privacy & Footprint Analysis
- Digital Footprint Score
- Privacy Score
- Ecosystem Concentration Metrics
- Tracking Surface Assessment

### 🔥 Exposure Heatmap
Visualizes estimated exposure across:
- Identity Data
- Location Data
- Social Graph
- Financial Data
- Behavioral Data
- Content Habits

### 🏢 Company Exposure Ranking
Ranks companies based on estimated user exposure.

### 📋 Data Collection Matrix
Shows the types of data different services may collect.

### 🎯 Risk Radar
Interactive radar visualization of privacy risks.

### 🤖 Digital Twin Profile
AI-generated behavioral profile based on application usage patterns.

### 💰 Valuable Data Assets
Highlights the most commercially valuable user information.

### ⚙️ Privacy Improvement Simulator
Allows users to simulate privacy-enhancing actions and observe score improvements.

### 📑 Final Verdict
Summarizes privacy strengths, risks, and recommendations.
