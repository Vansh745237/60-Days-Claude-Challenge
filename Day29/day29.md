# Day 29 – Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## 🚀 Overview

On **Day 29** of my **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I built **Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab**, an interactive supply chain crisis simulation using **HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React (CDN)**.

The simulator places users in the role of a business leader responsible for navigating unexpected supply chain disruptions. Players must make strategic decisions, negotiate with suppliers, answer leadership questions, invest in AI technologies, and analyze business outcomes through an executive dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Simulate real-world supply chain disruptions.
* Improve strategic decision-making.
* Understand supplier negotiations and leadership responsibilities.
* Explore AI-powered supply chain solutions.
* Evaluate business performance after managing a crisis.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🏢 Random Company Profile Generator
* ⚠️ Dynamic Supply Chain Crisis Scenarios
* 🎯 War Room Decision Making
* 📊 Live Business Performance Metrics
* 🤝 Supplier Negotiation Simulation
* 👨‍💼 CEO Leadership Assessment
* 🤖 AI Strategy Investment Selection
* 📈 Executive Dashboard with Final Business Score
* 🔄 Replay with Different Scenarios
