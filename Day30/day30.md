# Day 30 – Supply Chain Builder

## 🚀 Overview

On **Day 30** of my **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I built **Supply Chain Builder**, an interactive learning application that introduces the fundamentals of supply chain management through a beginner-friendly simulation.

The application explains every concept before asking users to make decisions, helping them understand how supply chains work and how business choices impact cost, delivery, risk, customer satisfaction, and sustainability.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Learn the basics of supply chain management.
* Build a supply chain step by step.
* Understand the trade-offs behind business decisions.
* Visualize how different choices affect overall business performance.
* Explore real-world supply chain strategies through an interactive simulation.

---

## ✨ Features

* 📖 Beginner-friendly introduction to supply chains
* 🏢 Random company generation for every playthrough
* 🌍 Factory location selection
* 🤝 Supplier strategy (Single vs Multiple Suppliers)
* 🏬 Warehouse strategy selection
* 🚚 Transportation method comparison
* 📦 Inventory strategy planning
* 📊 Live business metrics with animated progress bars
* 📈 Final enterprise dashboard with performance analysis
* 💡 Personalized strengths, weaknesses, risks, and improvement recommendations
* 🔄 Replay with a new company and scenario
