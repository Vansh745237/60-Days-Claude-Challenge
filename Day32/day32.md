# Day 32 – Think Like a Marketing Strategist: Grow This Brand

## 🚀 Overview

On **Day 32** of my **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I built **Think Like a Marketing Strategist: Grow This Brand**, an interactive learning application that teaches beginners how marketers think when building a business or personal brand.

Instead of generating marketing content, the simulator focuses on strategic thinking by guiding users through audience research, platform selection, content strategy, growth planning, and real-world marketing decisions. Each section includes beginner-friendly explanations and reusable Claude prompts to help users improve both marketing and prompt engineering skills.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand the fundamentals of marketing strategy.
* Learn how to analyze a business or personal brand.
* Select the right marketing channels for different goals.
* Build a content strategy with clear content pillars.
* Create a 30-day strategic marketing roadmap.
* Practice responding to real-world marketing events.
* Generate a personalized marketing growth report.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🎯 Three learning modes:

  * Use My Own Business
  * Build My Personal Brand
  * Random Client Generator
* 🏢 Random business profile generation
* 👥 Audience analysis with simple explanations
* 📱 Social media platform selection
* 📚 Content pillar selection
* 🗓️ 30-Day marketing roadmap
* ⚡ Random marketing events with decision outcomes
* 📊 Personalized Growth Report
* 🤖 "How to Ask Claude" prompt engineering cards
* 📈 Progress tracker
* 🎨 Responsive modern dark UI
* 🔄 Replay with new scenarios
