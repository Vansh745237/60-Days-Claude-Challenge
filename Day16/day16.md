# Day 16 – Stock Fundamental Research Skill

## Overview

This project demonstrates the creation and testing of a reusable **Claude Custom Skill** called **stock-fundamental-research**. The skill is designed to analyze Indian and global listed companies using fundamental analysis, valuation metrics, financial statements, ownership trends, business quality, growth prospects, and risk assessment.

The objective was to build a reusable workflow that can generate structured stock research reports without repeatedly entering the same prompt.

---

## Skill Details

### Skill Name
`stock-fundamental-research`

### Description
Analyze Indian and global listed companies using fundamentals, financial statements, business quality, competitive advantages, valuation, risks, and growth prospects. Generate evidence-based research reports and investor-friendly summaries. Never provide direct buy, sell, or hold recommendations.
