# 📄 Day 11 - ATS Resume Analysis & Optimization Report

## 📌 Overview
The ATS Resume Analysis & Optimization Report evaluates a resume against a target job role to determine its compatibility with Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). The report identifies existing keywords, missing skills, optimization opportunities, and provides actionable recommendations to improve resume visibility and recruiter acceptance rates.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze resume ATS compatibility.
- Measure keyword alignment with job descriptions.
- Identify missing technical and professional skills.
- Improve resume readability and ATS performance.
- Generate a professional ATS evaluation report.

---
